About the Firm

Not just strategy.
Systems that work.

Cyan Consulting was founded on a simple observation: most institutions know what they want to build, but very few know how to build it at scale. Governments commission strategies that never become programs. Universities redesign curricula that never reach employers. Innovation hubs launch without the talent pipelines that would make them thrive.

We close that gap. We work at the intersection of policy, people, and execution — designing the ecosystems, talent pipelines, and national programs that make development goals real. We've built across governments, international donors, universities, and private sector partners across Africa. We don't just advise. We stay until it works.

Our edge is simple: we've done it before, at scale. 60,000+ people trained. Hubs built across multiple markets. Funding raised for early-stage ventures. Partnerships brokered across actors who don't naturally align. That track record is the foundation of everything we offer.

Our Approach

How we
work.

Every engagement follows the same disciplined arc. We don't skip steps, and we don't stop at strategy.

01
Diagnostic
We spend time understanding the real system — not just the stated problem. Stakeholder interviews, ecosystem mapping, gap analysis, and global benchmarking.

Design
